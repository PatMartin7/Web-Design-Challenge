Latitudes of Weather

How does a place's Latitude vs the Equator affect its weather? Temperatures go up the nearer you are to the equator and go down the farther you get right? But why doesn't it snow in Australia like it does in Siberia? IS there more to this relationship than meets the eye? Lt's find out!

Using weather data from the OpenWeather API and through the power of Python and HTML, we will take a look at what effect lattitude has on the following: Max Temp, Humidity, Cloudiness and Wind Speed to try and get deeper insight on the affect of Latitude
